FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about addresses, scanning, and compliance. Contact us if you need anything more specific.

Can I use this address as my Registered Office?

Yes, if you choose a plan that includes Registered Office service. We will receive and process official mail from Companies House and HMRC on your behalf.

Do you open my mail by default?

For scanning and digital mailroom services, we open mail in our secure facility to create PDF copies. If you only require forwarding, we can forward sealed items according to your preferences.

Can non-UK residents use this service?

Yes. Many of our clients are based overseas but need a compliant UK address. Additional ID documentation may be required depending on your country of residence.

What identification do you require?

We will ask for standard KYC documentation such as a government-issued photo ID and proof of address. All documents are handled and stored securely.

How long does it take to set everything up?

In most cases, your address is ready as soon as payment is complete and your ID has been verified. Many customers are fully set up within one working day.
