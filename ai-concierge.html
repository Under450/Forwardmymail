Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get started? +
Most businesses are up and running within 30 minutes. Simply choose your plan, customize your settings, and forward your calls. No technical expertise required!
Does it really sound human? +
Yes! Our advanced AI uses natural language processing and premium voice synthesis to create conversations that are virtually indistinguishable from human receptionists. Most callers don't realize they're speaking with AI.
Can I customize the responses? +
Absolutely! You can customize greetings, responses, and even the AI's personality to match your brand. Premium plans include custom voice branding options.
What happens if the AI can't answer a question? +
The AI is designed to handle most common inquiries, but if it encounters something outside its knowledge base, it can take a message, schedule a callback, or transfer to a human team member seamlessly.
Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime? +
All our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime with no penalties or fees.
