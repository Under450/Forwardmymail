Terms & Conditions

1. Service Agreement

By using Forward My Mail services, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

2. Credit System

All credits are NON-REFUNDABLE. Credits expire after 6 MONTHS from purchase date. Service activates IMMEDIATELY upon purchase. NO extensions, rollovers, or refunds are provided under any circumstances.

  • First page scan: 0.50
  • Additional pages: 0.25 each
  • Storage (after 30 days): 0.50/month per item

3. Storage Timeline (60-Day Lifecycle)

Days 1-30: FREE storage period. You must choose an action (Scan/Post/Keep/Shred) before this period ends.

Day 25: First warning email sent - 5 days of free storage remaining.

Day 30: Paid storage begins at 0.50/month. If you have insufficient credits, a debt record is created.

Day 55: Final warning - mail will be automatically shredded in 5 days if no action is taken.

Day 60: Mail is automatically shredded. Outstanding balances remain payable.

4. Registered Office Package

The Registered Office package includes FREE scans from HMRC and Companies House. All other mail is charged at standard rates.

If you have outstanding debts and mail is auto-shredded, we reserve the right to notify Companies House to remove your registered office address from our location.

5. Liability

Our liability is limited to a maximum of 100 per incident. We are not responsible for mail loss due to postal service errors, force majeure, or items not collected within the 60-day period.

6. Prohibited Items

We do not accept hazardous materials, illegal items, perishables, or items requiring special handling.

7. Account Suspension

Accounts with outstanding debts may be suspended until payment is received. Registered Office customers with debts will have their registered office address removed.
