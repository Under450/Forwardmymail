Tips for a successful retry
💡

Use good lighting

Find a bright, well-lit area. Avoid backlighting from windows behind you.

📷

Keep your ID flat and fully visible

All four corners of your document must be clearly in the frame with no glare or shadows.

🤳

Face the camera directly for your selfie

Look straight ahead, remove glasses if possible, and ensure your full face is visible.

🛂

Use a valid, in-date document

Passport or driving licence — must be current and not expired.
